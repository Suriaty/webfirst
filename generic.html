About Me

My name is Siti Nur Suriaty Binti Rosdi, 23 years old. Stay at Kampung Mak Chili Kemaman. I am the second child of six siblings. It can be said that I am the hope of my parents and younger siblings.

My Journey

  • SPM, 2017 in SMK Sultan Ismail 2
  • STPM, 2019 in SMK Sultan Ismail
  • SKM 02 Multimedia, 2020 in IKBN Kemasik
  • Bachelor Digital Graphic Design in process at UCTATI

Disappointed with STPM UPU result

I aspire to continue my studies at university. However, after failing in the UPU application during STPM it made me down and discouraged to continue my education. So I decided to work in retail. For example waitresses and fruit shop seller. However, some period after that I felt bored by doing the same thing every day. Nothing can I improve in myself. So I decide to continue learning to gain knowledge and new things every day. Coincidentally, at that time IKBN Kemasik was open for student recruitment so I continued to apply and study at there.

Having a degree is a must!

As a child (older sister), I need to be a role model to my younger siblings. I have to make my parents proud of my academic success. Therefore, I decided to continue my studies at the bachelor level at UCTATI. Armed with an interest in the field of graphic editing, I continued to choose the BDGD program as my track to achieve my dreams.
